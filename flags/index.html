Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Does the interest form commit me to buying?

No. Filling out the form does not require payment today and does not lock you into an order. It only lets me know you may be interested.

When is payment due?

Once orders officially open, a $50 deposit reserves your flag. The remaining $200 is due at pickup or delivery.

How can I pay?

Venmo, PayPal, and cash are accepted.

Can the flag stay outside?

It is protected with interior/exterior polyurethane, but it is best kept indoors or in a covered area away from direct exposure to rain, sun, and other elements.

Is hanging hardware included?

Yes. A French cleat is installed on the back, and wall anchors, screws, and an alignment template are included.

How will I receive my flag?

We’ll coordinate pickup or local delivery individually. A public meeting spot is always an option; home pickup or delivery may be arranged when we know each other personally.
